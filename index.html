About Us

InTech Engineering Services (IES) introduces itself as one of the fast growing company for Oil and Gas Pipeline projects. We have extensive experience in design, supply, laying, jointing, testing and commissioning of bulk and distribution pipeline network, with latest technology of HDD. IES is an established player in the field of construction of Oil/Product Cross-Country Pipelines; Terminals and associated facilities constituting Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation Works; City Gas Distribution Networks and Water Pipelines.
