About Us

InTech Engineering Services (IES) introduces itself as one of the fast growing company for Oil and Gas Pipeline projects. We have extensive experience in design, supply, laying, jointing, testing and commissioning of bulk and distribution pipeline network, with latest technology of HDD. IES is an established player in the field of construction of Oil / Product Cross-Country Pipelines; Terminals and associated facilities constituting Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation Works; City Gas Distribution Networks and Water Pipelines. We are one of the pioneers Company in the field of Pipeline Installation and Trenchless Solution for Oil and Gas, Telecommunication, Sewerage and Water Works and undertake projects on trunk basis. We have the pride and privilege of having esteemed organizations of the Indian Industry in both, the private and public sector, as our clients. We strive to complete all our projects on time with strict HSE measures. Safety, Quality and Client satisfaction are our key concerns. We are well equipped with experienced techno-commercial technocrats, tools & tackles, equipment and machineries.
